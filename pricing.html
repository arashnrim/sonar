FAQ

What is Sonar?

Sonar is a music streaming service that allows you to listen to music in a new way. We use spatial audio to create a more immersive listening experience.

How much does it cost?

Sonar is a pay-as-you-go service. You pay for the amount of music you listen to. The more you listen, the more you pay.

What's the range of costs I can expect paying using Sonar?

You can expect to pay as little as $0.50 per month and as much as $14.60 a month. Use our calculator above to see how much you can expect to pay.

Are there discounts available?

Yes. If you're a student or a member of the military, you can get a discount. Depending on how much you are able to pay, we'll work with you to make a discount that works.
