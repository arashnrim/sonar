What's Sonar like?

Time-synced lyrics, to the word.

Sonar offers accurate lyrics from artists via LyricFind, so you'll be able to play your music and sing to it. We're also introducing word-by-word-timed lyrics, so you can have a karaoke experience like no other.

Crystal-clear context behind every line.

Artists know their songs best and what every line means. We work with Genius and other services to scout for what artists have said about their songs and present them to you in an understandable manner.

Understand songs, even if they're not your native language.

Sonar uses community-powered translations by Musixmatch and sourced internally to provide you with the best translations for the songs you listen to. You don't need to copy and paste song lyrics into a translator anymore.
